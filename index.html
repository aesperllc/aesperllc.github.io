Furia Rising

An incremental mobile game on iOS/Android, where you can manage a team of "Risers". The goal is to accumulate resources, unlock more 'Risers', mine deeper, and get the best equips in order to make them mine faster and craft better equipment (6 rarities). It boasts a unique graphics look that is not often seen for mobile games, with sprite-lighting, day/night cycle, and particle effects. It was developed and actually has so much features and little details in it that makes it fun to play on a commute, at night. Additionally, while you are offline, you still gain some meownies, experience and soul crystals!

It is an incremental, clicker game that has 12 different resources, 20 different characters, and a crazy crafting system. When you mine rocks long enough, you will unlock 'Relics', which will grant you an active ability! This may be 20% critical chance, or something else!

Each time you level up, you can spend four skill points, and these could be given to walk speed, attack damage, critical strike, etc. You can also spend the main currency to level up these skills to an extent, or even level up the Risers themselves. The risers tend to specialize - one may have super fast attack speed, a lot of ore capacity, or high damage. In this way, you start to create a 'build' that makes your game unique. We also have a real time leaderboard, so make sure to sign up when you first log in, instead of being a 'guest'. Enjoy!

The game will eventually have a major update that is accessible if you are able to mine to the bottom mining shaft, that includes a mystery level, could be PvE, open world, but it is TBD.
